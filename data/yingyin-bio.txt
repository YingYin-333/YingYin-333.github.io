Ying Yin was born in Jiujiang, China, in 1995. She lived and studied in this city until graduating from high school in 2014. In September 2014, she entered Nanchang University to pursue her bachelor’s degree in E-Commerce. After four years, she graduated cum laude and continued her master’s degree at Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT). During her time at BIT, she was supervised by Prof. Dr. Lun Ran. It was then that she began learning how to conduct scientific research and decided to pursue a career in academia. In December 2020, she moved to Leiden, the Netherlands, and joined the Science-Based Business Group to pursue her PhD in operations management. She was supervised by Prof. dr. S. Jong Kon Chin, Prof. dr. R.A. Zuidwijk, and Dr. X. Li. Her research interests include capacity planning and shared-based platform operations.
